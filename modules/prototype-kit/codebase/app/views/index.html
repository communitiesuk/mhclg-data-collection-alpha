Welcome to CORE

CORE is the COntinuous REcording of Lettings and Sales in Social Housing in England.

This service provides accurate and timely information about new social housing lettings, sales, tenants and buyers across England.

The information recorded on this portal is used by government bodies and organisations to help guide regulatory and housing policy decisions.

This site lets you:

  • learn about CORE and how its data is used
  • submit and update CORE logs
  • access CORE data for reporting and analysis
